SYNRC PACK 2019

2GB USB

The SYNRC PACK is a list of repositories with full history as a distribution package written onto 2GB USB storage device. It also contains formal models of N2O in Standard ML, Haskell, Agda, and Lean 4. More languages yet to come at n2o.tech.

Every Erlang project is buildable with mad, mix, rebar3, has its own hex.pm publication, and some of them are hosted at n2o.cloud.
